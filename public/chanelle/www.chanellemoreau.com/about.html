 About The Artist

 

Born and raised on the island of Maui in Hawai’i, nature from the beginning has been a guiding force for all my creative endeavors. These tropical color palettes paired with organic elements are an ongoing inspiration and theme throughout the whole of my work.

I began sketching, painting, and playing with cameras from early childhood. These exercises and experiments in personal expression drove me to chase after a life where these passions could become my life’s work.

With no formal training, nor a degree, over the years I’ve learned to teach myself the fundamentals of graphic and web design, film and digital photography, videography, illustration, painting, writing, and more. This hands-on approach alongside an intuitive sense of aesthetics and my personal style are where I’ve found my home in the realm of creativity. These are my guides to storytelling through the arts.
